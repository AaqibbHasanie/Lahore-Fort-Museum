Exquisite Mughal Art in the exterior

The exterior of Mughal Art Gallery also represents an extra-ordinary view which immediately reminds a spectator about the Mughal empire. Mughal art gallery also reminds how Mughal designers considered every minute details and produced masterpieces.

Mughal Gallery is surrounded by lush green gardens which are taken care of regularly. An amazing thing about Mughal gallery is its location since one can easily reach other galleries such as "Sikh Art Gallery" and "Armory Gallery" easily from its location and explore the hidden treasures preserved in these galleries.
